IZICI
- Izibali eziningi: igama, ithagethi (uyazikhethela), incazelo, umbala/isithonjana, imodi yokudumisa.
- Ukwenyuka okusheshayo/ukuncipha/ukusetha kabusha: izindawo zokuthinta ezinkulu zokufinyelela ngesandla esisodwa.
- Inqubekelaphambili eqondiwe: amaphesenti, asele, inani lemijikelezo (izinyathelo ezingama-33/99 kwimodi yokudumisa).
- Ama-Haptics: ukudlidliza okufushane ngesinyathelo ngasinye, ukudlidliza okude ekuqedeni okuqondiwe/emathangeni.
- Gcina isikrini sivuliwe (uyazikhethela): amaseshini amade.
- Hlehlisa: lungisa ukuthinta okungalungile ngokuthi Hlehlisa phakathi nemizuzwana emi-5.
- Umlando kanye nezibalo: irekhodi leseshini elinesitembu sesikhathi; izifinyezo zansuku zonke/zeviki.
- Izikhumbuzi: isaziso nsuku zonke / masonto onke; Yeqa ngokuzenzakalelayo phakathi namahora athule.
- Itimu: emnyama/ekhanyayo/isistimu.
- Izikhangiso: isibhengezo / isibonisi sesikhangiso somdabu ngaphakathi kwesicelo.
- Ubumfihlo: awekho ama-akhawunti, akukho ukulandelela; Idatha igcinwa kusizindalwazi sendawo (Igumbi).

SEBENZISA AMACEBISO
- Uma imodi ye-tasbih ivuliwe, ukuzungezisa kuyanda ngazo zonke izinyathelo ezingama-33 futhi kunikeza ukudlidliza okude.
- Iphesenti/insalela ibalwa ngokuzenzakalelayo ekubaleni lapho ubeka khona umgomo.
- "Gcina isikrini" sisetshenziswa kuphela esikrinini semininingwane yemitha.
- Finyelela nciphisa/setha kabusha/hlela/gcina kungobo yomlando imisebenzi kusuka kumenyu yokucindezela okude.
- Phatha isikhathi/inkathi yesikhumbuzi kanye nesikhawu sesikhathi esithulile kusuka kokuthi Izilungiselelo.

UBUMFIHLO
- Idatha igcinwa kudivayisi; Ayikho idatha yomuntu siqu, indawo noma imininingwane yokuxhumana eqoqwe.
- Uxhumano lwe-inthanethi lusetshenziswa kuphela ukulayisha izikhangiso.

IMPENDULO
- Ungathumela impendulo ngamaphutha/iziphakamiso ngokuhlolwa kwesitolo.
